Our Services

At Telecare Services, we provide a wide range of specialized care solutions designed to empower individuals with IDD to lead independent and fulfilling lives.

Personal Support Services

Our personal support services are tailored to meet the individual needs of each participant. We offer comprehensive assistance to foster independence and improve daily living skills. This can include help with personal care, meal preparation, medication reminders, or learning how to navigate the local community safely.

Supported Living Services

Our supported living services aim to create a home environment where individuals can thrive while receiving the necessary support to lead fulfilling lives within their communities. These services focus on empowering individuals to live as independently as possible in their own homes, while having support available when needed.

Nursing Support Services

Our nursing support services ensure that participants receive specialized care and medical attention as needed, delivered by qualified healthcare professionals. This includes health monitoring, medication management, and coordination with other medical providers to ensure holistic health outcomes.

Community Development Services (CDS)

CDS focuses on fostering community engagement, encouraging social inclusion, and promoting active participation within diverse community settings. We help individuals explore their interests, join local clubs, and build meaningful relationships within their neighborhoods.

Respite Care

We provide temporary relief for caregivers through respite care services. These services offer short-term breaks, allowing caregivers to recharge while ensuring continuous care for participants. Whether it’s for a few hours or a few days, we provide a safe and nurturing environment.

Employment Services

At Telecare Services, we are committed to empowering individuals with disabilities by providing comprehensive employment services. Our goal is to ensure that each individual finds meaningful, long-term employment. We offer job development, training, and ongoing support to both the employee and employer.
