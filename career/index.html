Join Our Team

At Telecare Services, we’re dedicated to transforming lives and creating a positive impact in the lives of individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities. Our team is comprised of passionate professionals committed to delivering exceptional care and support, empowering participants to lead fulfilling lives within their communities.

Why Telecare Services?

Discover a career that offers more than just a paycheck.

Meaningful Work

Working at Telecare means being part of a mission-driven organization focused on making a difference. Every day, our team members contribute to enhancing the lives of individuals with disabilities, fostering independence, and promoting inclusivity.

Professional Growth

We believe in investing in our team’s development. At Telecare, you’ll have opportunities for professional growth, training, and advancement, enabling you to expand your skills and expertise while making a real impact.

Collaborative Environment

Join a collaborative and supportive work environment where teamwork, respect, and open communication are valued. Our inclusive culture encourages diverse perspectives and ideas to better serve our participants.
