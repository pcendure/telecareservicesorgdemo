About Us

Telecare Services is more than just a provider of home-based and community-focused care for individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities (IDD). We are a community of dedicated professionals committed to making a difference in the lives of those we serve.

Our mission is simple: to empower each participant to live fulfilling, independent lives within their communities. We believe that everyone deserves the opportunity to reach their full potential and actively participate in the world around them.

Our Mission Statement

The mission of Telecare Services is to empower individuals with intellectual and developmental disabilities to lead fulfilling, independent lives by providing compassionate, person-centered support in their homes and communities.

Our Vision Statement

To be the leading catalyst for positive change in the lives of individuals with IDD, creating a world where every participant is valued, included, and empowered to achieve their dreams.

Our Community

Moments of joy, connection, and care.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide our care and define our community.

Compassion

We approach every individual we serve with empathy and kindness, ensuring they feel heard and respected.

Inclusion

We believe in creating a community where everyone is welcome, regardless of ability, and valued for their unique contributions.

Empowerment

We provide the support and resources needed for individuals to reach their goals and take charge of their own lives.

Excellence

We strive to provide the highest quality of care and support through continuous learning and dedication.

"Empowering lives, building communities."

Our Motto guides everything we do.
